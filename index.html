Journal

Welcome to my journey beyond the end. This space contains fragments of memories and spells collected throughout centuries of wandering—a magical chronicle of encounters both fleeting and profound.

I study magic to understand humanity, and humanity to understand magic. The centuries pass quickly when you're searching for the perfect spell, yet certain moments remain eternally vivid in memory.

"The world simply goes on without you. That's how it is for those who are left behind."

The pursuit of understanding continues. Perhaps the answers lie in the memories we cherish, rather than the knowledge we acquire.

Memories fade, but feelings linger beyond time.
